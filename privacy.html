Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 18, 2026

At Chat With Santa, we take your privacy and the privacy of your children very seriously. We have designed our app to be a safe, magical experience without compromising personal data.

1. Information We Collect

We believe in minimal data collection. The Chat With Santa app does not require you to create an account. Any data processed by the app (such as voice recordings or text inputs) is used solely to generate a response from Santa in real-time.

  • Voice Data: Voice inputs are processed momentarily to convert speech to text. We do not store, save, or share your audio recordings.
  • Text Data: Chat logs are stored locally on your device so you can review past conversations. We do not transmit these logs to our servers.
  • Analytics: We may collect anonymous, aggregated crash reports and usage statistics to help us improve the app. This data cannot be linked back to you.

2. Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

Our application is designed for families, including children. We are fully compliant with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA). We do not knowingly collect, store, or solicit personally identifiable information from children under the age of 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us immediately so we can delete it.

3. Artificial Intelligence & Local Processing

To provide the magic of Santa's replies, our application relies entirely on on-device local processing. We use cutting-edge, secure technology including Google's Gemma model and Apple Intelligence to generate conversations locally on your device. Since all AI generation occurs directly on your hardware, no conversation text, voice audio, or child profile data is sent to external servers or used to train external AI models.

4. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
